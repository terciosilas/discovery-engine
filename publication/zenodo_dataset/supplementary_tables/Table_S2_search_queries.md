## Inclusion Criteria (defined a priori)

- Language: English
- Publication types: Journal article, Review, Systematic review, Meta-analysis, Preprint
- Date: 2020-01-01 to 2026-12-31
- Must address at least ONE biological theme (aging, longevity, senescence, geroprotection, healthspan, lifespan) AND at least ONE molecular theme (protein, proteomics, drug, compound, inhibitor, agonist)
- Abstract must be available

## Exclusion Criteria (defined a priori)

- Publication types: Retracted, Erratum, Editorial, Letter to editor, Case report
- Topics: Cosmetic aging, food aging, material aging, wine aging, cheese aging
- Minimum quality: Abstract >= 50 words, Title >= 5 words
